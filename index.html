WHO I AM

Hey! I’m Zach Nagatani. I’m a guy from California, a husband to an incredible woman, a cat-dad, a musician... and I’m a front-end web developer.

I’m passionate about the web and I believe that it’s an amazing tool and resource that can be used to build businesses, gain knowledge, and even change people’s lives. And when it comes to my craft, I care deeply about good code, clean design, and an awesome experience for anyone who uses my projects.

WHAT I DO

Web Development

Like I said: I'm a front-end web developer. That means that I create the part of the web that users interact with. In particular, I build interactive web applications, as well as custom websites. I'm skilled in the languages of the web: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm always keeping up on the latest technologies.

Web Design

When it comes to client work, my focus is designing and developing custom websites that meet your needs. In other words, I listen to you and come up with a solution on the web tailor-made for your business. If you want, I'll then convert your site into a custom WordPress theme, allowing you the freedom to update your site's content without needing to hire a developer every time.
